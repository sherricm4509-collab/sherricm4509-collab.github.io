Maine Coon is one of the oldest breeds in North America. They are originally from Maine and known at the “gentle giant”. They can weigh between 9 and 18 pounds but males can exceed 20 pounds.
Their coats are thick and water resistant. They are large and their paws are round with little tufts of fur between the toes to help them walk on snow.
Maine Coons are affectionate and dog-like cats. They make good companions and are fun to play with. They can also be trained to follow some commands and perform tricks.
They do not require a lot of grooming due to them self-maintaining. They also don’t need any special diets but a high protein diet does better for them because of their larger size.
